# Product Vision

## Objective
Provide governance and management capabilities on top of Apache APISIX.

## Core Principles
- APISIX handles traffic.
- Spring Boot handles governance.
- Plugins remain loosely coupled.
- Everything is auditable.
- Everything is rollbackable.

## Phase 1
- Service Management
- Upstream Management
- Route Management
- Plugin Management

## Phase 2
- Consumer Management
- SSL Management
- Deployment Management

## Phase 3
- Approval Workflow
- Versioning
- Audit
- Developer Portal
